Welcome To Rio Royale Resort – Your Perfect Getaway In Karjat, Near Mumbai & Pune

Escape to Rio Royale Resort, the best resort in Karjat, where luxury meets nature in a peaceful valley surrounded by lush hills and rivers. Designed for families, couples, and groups, it’s the ideal weekend getaway near Mumbai and Pune.

Unwind in our serene resort where comfort, adventure, and nature come together. From a relaxing poolside retreat to outdoor fun and a delightful petting zoo, Rio Royale promises a perfect blend of leisure and excitement for every guest.

Find your ideal escape in our beautifully designed rooms and suites — crafted for your comfort with modern amenities and a cozy touch of nature. Every stay at Rio Royale Resort Karjat is an experience of warmth, joy, and togetherness.

Reconnect with the outdoors through our unique agro-tourism experiences, thrilling resort activities, and nature walks — all within a scenic landscape that feels worlds away from the city.
